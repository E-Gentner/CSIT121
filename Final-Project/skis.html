My Personal Collection

On this page I will show you each of the skis I own and have used throughout the years of my time skiing. I will tell you some basic stats and my opinion on them if you should want to ride them.

Rossignol Experience 80 Ci

The Rossignol Experience 80 Ci was the first ski I ever bought for myself once I was done growing so it will always have a place in my heart. The one I ride is 167 cm tall. With a waist of 80 cm, which is where the 80 in the name comes from. It has a honeycomb tip to reduce the swing weight and a partial sidewall to get more grip when you engage in your turns. And it also has carbon inlays which give it a little bit more rigidity while still being pretty light and also offering some springiness into the ski.
